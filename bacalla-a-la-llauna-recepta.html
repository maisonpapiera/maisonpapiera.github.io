Receta BACALAO A LA LLAUNA (Cataluña) Mis Recetas Caseras

Bacallà a la llauna. Bacallà a la llauna is a traditional Spanish fish dish originating from Barcelona and dating back to the 19th century. The dish is made with a combination of salt cod, flour, garlic, paprika or pimentón, parsley, and olive oil. The salt cod is desalted, drained, dried, and the fillets are then dredged in flour and fried.
